AI Short Course

Instructor: Hatim A. Hamid

Welcome to the AI Short Course! This course is designed for individuals interested in learning the basics of AI and exploring the power of ChatGPT. The course consists of six sessions, providing comprehensive knowledge and practical experience in the field of AI.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 sessions
  • Timing: After hours, suitable for working professionals
  • Location: AIC Meeting Rooms, Newport, Victoria
  • Email for Reservations: enquiries@hatim.a.hamid.au

Session Topics

Session 1: Introduction to AI

In this session, we will cover the basics of artificial intelligence, its applications, and its impact on various industries.

Session 2: Fundamentals of Machine Learning

This session will dive into the fundamental concepts of machine learning, including supervised and unsupervised learning, data preprocessing, and model training.

Session 3: Natural Language Processing

Explore the fascinating world of natural language processing (NLP) and learn how AI systems can understand and generate human language.

Session 4: Neural Networks and Deep Learning

Discover the inner workings of neural networks and delve into deep learning algorithms that power AI breakthroughs.

Session 5: Computer Vision

Gain insights into computer vision techniques, including image recognition, object detection, and image generation.

Session 6: ChatGPT and Practical AI Applications

In the final session, we will explore the power of ChatGPT and learn how to apply AI in real-world scenarios through hands-on projects.
